Statement

Alec Hales is an artist primarily concerned with creating visual networks stretching beyond representation. Hybrid forms find themselves in labyrinth-like spaces. Understanding of the real and unreal breaks down as animal, vegetable and cosmic beings merge — often seen in a process of change and becoming, exhibiting or receiving acts of aggression and inversion.

His images are laden with existential and conceptual concerns around the experience of the contemporary subject in a technologically mediated space-time. Reimagining imagery from futurism and expressionism, his paintings put into question optimism about technological progress and the individual. They can be humorous, illogical and severe.
